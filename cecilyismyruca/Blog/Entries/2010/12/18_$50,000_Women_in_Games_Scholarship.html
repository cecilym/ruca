$50,000 Women in Games Scholarship

 

I found out about a thick, juicy scholarship for women in games from the Women In Games facebook page. Apparently it’s the first scholarship of its kind. Thank you, thank you, thank you to the Vancouver film school and all of the sponsors who made the scholarship possible. Even if I don’t win, I get teary thinking of the opportunities I want to create for other girls someday. Scholarship info here.
