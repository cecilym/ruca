Rules for Renegades

 

I am in San Francisco for Jeff and Kane’s conference, Mastering Wealth Through Relationships, aka “Release Your Inner Rockstar”. Kane has been mentoring me with my ongoing projects in the music and game industry and he set me up for a little meeting today with Christine Comaford, the badass internationally renowned business woman who’s early accomplishments include making 10 million in tech without even graduating from high school. To get ready for our meeting I read her book, Rules for Renegads, which I totally recommend. She gives away some reeeeaaally useful business planning documents here on this website:


http://rulesforrenegades.com/resources.html


I just got out of our meeting and I am so honored to have met this inspiring woman. She gave me some great advice about my game that I will definitely be implementing. Now, back to Jeff and Kane’s conference... I’ll let you know how it goes.
