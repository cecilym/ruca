Chop Suey TONIGHT

 

Seattle Music Promoters Calendar +

Lorn, Mochipet, the Great Mundane

 

Tonight I’m gonna see Lorn, Mochipet, the Great Mundane and some of cats are spin at Chop Suey. The last times I saw Lorn and Mochipet the dance party was epic so I’m definitely looking forward to the show. There’s a lot going on town tonight but I still think we are gonna pack the house. Shlomo is doing an after hours at Space so that will be a cool way to bring everyone together who didn’t want to miss his set.


A lot of people were complaining that Seattle music promoters were booking competing shows on the same nights so I created a public Seattle Music Promoters calendar on google. Yea, there’s nwtekno, but it seems to me a cluttered information source for people on the planning side of the industry. I thought a google calendar would be a simpler, more elegant way for people in the industry to get brass tacks info they should know before booking their events.  Promoters can post artists, venue, set times, label/promo company, etc. Here is a link to my calendar:
