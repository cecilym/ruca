Splatinum’s Know Us remix is BLUE CHIP

 

Splatinum’s remix of Know Us by Love and Light is incredible and definitely the best Know Us remix I’ve heard yet. I got to see Andrew making a lot of it at home and from very early on in the production work you could tell it was going to be a blue chip track. I especially appreciate the sonic space in the remix because it allows their silky production technique to breathe while also making every kick seem to hit that much harder.


As a production force, Splatinum is already known as funky, progressive, and dramatic, but their recent tracks reveal a refined elegance that takes bass music to a whole new level.


Big ups, Splatinum. You guys are the shit and you continue to impress me with every new release.
