Welcome to Indiex, located in the 7th dimension, whose life forms are dependent on a wide spectrum of sound waves for survival. The majority of Indiex native population are Deaddy Bears, a form of intelligence not found on Earth, whose lives and very biology are shaped by musical sound waves. In 7D, some concepts that are considered "abstract" on Earth are experienced as concrete realities. The Deaddy Bears' senses have evolved to be hyper aware of music sounds. In 7D, however, concepts such as tonality, key, Jazz, and Dubstep are experienced in physical forms. It is for this reason that Indiex is where rock stars go when they die, continuing to exist as physical manifestations of the sound waves with which their music and various aspects of their souls resonate.


If you were to describe this world in terms of a Role Playing Game, you would only be telling half of the story. If you were to describe this world in terms of a Social Game, you would be missing the point. The mechanics of game play are embedded within the ark of the story and can be determined by how one chooses to play the game. On the other hand, even describing this world as a game would be inaccurate because its very existence is a concrete depiction of what is really happening right now in abstract terms. Just as scientific models are accepted metaphors that accurately describe observed phenomena, this "game" models phenomena of music and culture as they are currently understood. Unlike scientific models, the game is dynamic, by its very nature redefining itself in real time to reflect changes in the real world. Allow me to show you around a little bit. You will see what I mean.
